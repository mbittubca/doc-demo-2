Here's a complete **single doctor clinic** frontend structure with detailed page content for the demo.[1][2][3]

## Pages List (6 Pages)

```
1. Home (Landing Page)
2. About Doctor
3. Services & Treatments
4. Book Appointment
5. Patient Resources
6. Contact
```

***

## Page-by-Page Content Breakdown

### 1. Home (Landing Page)[3][1]

**Hero Section**
- Doctor's professional photo with clinic interior background[1]
- Headline: "Compassionate Care, Expert Treatment"
- Subheadline: "Dr. [Name], [Specialization] - [Years] years of experience serving [City Name]"
- Two CTA buttons: "Book Appointment" (primary) and "Call Now" (secondary)[4][3]

**Quick Info Cards** (3-column grid)[4]
- **Working Hours**: Mon-Sat: 10 AM - 7 PM, Sun: Closed
- **Emergency Contact**: Phone number with click-to-call
- **Location**: Clinic address with "Get Directions" link

**Why Choose Dr. [Name]** (4-point section)[2][3]
- Board-certified [Specialization] with [X] years experience
- Advanced treatment techniques and modern equipment
- Personalized care plans for each patient
- Flexible appointment scheduling including weekend slots

**Services Overview** (Grid of 4-6 services)[5][3]
- Card format with icon, service name, 1-line description, and "Learn More" link
- Example: General Consultation, Preventive Care, Chronic Disease Management, Health Screenings

**Patient Testimonials** (Carousel slider with 3-5 reviews)[3][4]
- Patient name, location, 5-star rating
- Testimonial text (2-3 sentences)
- Real or stock patient photos

**Call-to-Action Section**[4]
- "Ready to Take Control of Your Health?"
- "Schedule Your Consultation Today"
- Appointment booking button + WhatsApp direct message button

### 4. Book Appointment[5][3][4]

**Page Header**
- "Schedule Your Visit"
- "Choose a convenient time - online booking available 24/7"[5]

**Appointment Booking Form**[4]

**Step 1: Select Service** (Dropdown)
- General Consultation
- Follow-up Visit
- Health Checkup
- Specific Treatment (dropdown list)

**Step 2: Choose Date** (Calendar widget)[5]
- Next 7-14 days visible
- Available dates highlighted in green
- Unavailable dates grayed out

**Step 3: Select Time Slot** (Grid of buttons)[5]
- Morning slots: 10:00 AM, 11:00 AM, 12:00 PM
- Afternoon slots: 2:00 PM, 3:00 PM, 4:00 PM
- Evening slots: 5:00 PM, 6:00 PM
- Available slots in blue, booked slots disabled

**Step 4: Patient Details**[4]
- Full Name (required)
- Phone Number (required)
- Email Address
- Age / Date of Birth
- Reason for Visit (textarea)
- First-time or returning patient (radio buttons)

**Step 5: Confirmation**
- Review booking details
- "Confirm Appointment" button
- WhatsApp confirmation option checkbox

**Alternative Booking Methods**[5]
- "Prefer to call? Contact us at [Phone Number]"
- WhatsApp booking button: "Book via WhatsApp"
- "Walk-ins welcome during clinic hours"

**Appointment Policies**[4]
- Cancellation policy (24 hours notice)
- Late arrival policy (15 minutes grace period)
- What to bring (ID, insurance, previous reports)

## Design Notes[2][1][5]

**Color Scheme**: Calming blues (#3498db), clean whites, trust-building greens with professional typography[1]

**Mobile Optimization**: All forms and booking interfaces fully functional on mobile devices[5]

**Loading Speed**: Optimized images under 100KB, lazy loading for below-fold content[2]

**Trust Signals**: Display medical certifications, years of experience, patient count served prominently[1][4]

This structure gives potential clients a complete view of the single-doctor clinic's digital presence while demonstrating booking functionality and professional credibility for the 30-50% demo milestone.[2][1][3]
